UI/UX Design

Employer wanted to create a unique agile management tool that does not exist in the market. The team had to brainstorm a solution to create an application from scratch by creating the marketing brand, web design, and making it unique from competitors. I led the UI/UX portion of this project and was responsible for all design related decisions and deliverables. I collaborated with the product manager, engineers, and Subject Matter Experts to to design this agile management tool, keeping the end user in mind throughout the process. The software was implemented and introduced to the company and will be branched out to clients as well. Below is an example of an "edit" function.

Problem:Local nonprofit wanted to rebrand and modernize current website

Challenges:Short deadline and the client had limited technical expertise

Solution:Use-Centered Design to conduct interviews, market research, and build wireframes, screens designs and clickable prototypes

Results:The client invited the team to an event to reveal the new website design to the organization
