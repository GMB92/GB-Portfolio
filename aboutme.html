About Me

My name is Gabriela Botura, I have a degree in Management Information Systems and Marketing from Ohio University. I have 5 years of professional experience in consulting with my skills ranging from back-end development and data analysis to process improvement.

My passion for the arts sparked at a young age when I realized how much I can express myself through different types of creative outlets. I became classicaly trained on the piano, I pursued painting classes, I perfected my geometric drawings, and smoothed my calligraphing.

Coming from a technical professional background, it is hard to showcase my creative mindset to produce a beautiful product for my clients. My goal is to transition to UI/UX design to incorporate two of the things I know best: technical knowledge and creativity. I want to use my technical skills to design with the front end in mind while tapping into my creative mind to create smart, functional, and visually appealing designs for my clients needs.
